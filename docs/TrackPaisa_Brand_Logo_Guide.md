# TrackPaisa — Brand, Color, Logo, and Design System

## Brand Summary

TrackPaisa is a privacy-first personal expense and income tracker for people who want to understand their money clearly without bank sync, cards, bills, or complicated finance workflows.

## Brand Positioning

### Brand Promise

> Track every rupee, without the clutter.

### Brand Personality

- Clear
- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Practical
- Modern
- Privacy-first
- Indian-user friendly

### Brand Keywords

- Rupee
- Track
- Simple
- Private
- Monthly clarity
- Spending awareness
- Salary and expense management

## App Name Review

### Name: TrackPaisa

TrackPaisa is strong because:

- It is easy to understand.
- It clearly communicates money tracking.
- “Paisa” gives it a relatable Indian identity.
- It is short enough for an app name.
- It sounds practical, not fake or over-branded.

### Risk

There may already be public usage of similar names. Before public launch, check:

- Domain availability
- Google Play listing
- App Store listing
- Trademark database
- Social media handles

### Safer Variants

If the exact name is not available, consider:

- TrackPaisa Solo
- TrackPaisa Personal
- TrackPaisa Ledger
- PaisaTrackr
- RupeeTrail
- SpendPaisa
- PaisaPulse

## Recommended Final Name

Use **TrackPaisa** as the working name now. Before production launch, verify trademark and domain availability.

## Logo Direction

### Logo Concept 1 — Coin + Trend Line

A rounded coin shape with a clean upward/downward tracking line inside. This represents money movement and spending insights.

### Logo Concept 2 — Rupee + Path

A simplified ₹ symbol with a small path/line showing tracking and flow.

### Logo Concept 3 — Ledger Mark

A rounded square icon with three ledger lines and a small dot/rupee indicator.

## Recommended Logo Concept

Use **Coin + Trend Line** for the primary logo because it is:

- Simple
- Easy to recognize
- Good for app icon
- Works at small sizes
- Finance-related without looking like a bank

## Logo Usage

## Typography


## UI Style

### Use

- Soft backgrounds
- Rounded panels
- Clean dividers
- High contrast text
- Simple iconography
- Large touch targets
- Clear empty states

### Avoid

- Too many gradients
- Neon crypto look
- Overloaded dashboard
- Heavy shadows everywhere
- Tiny financial tables on mobile
- Too much text on first screen

## Voice and Copy

Use simple, friendly, clear copy.

Examples:

- “Your month at a glance”
- “Where your money went”
- “Add income”
- “Add expense”
- “Back up your data”
- “Restore from backup”
- “No account needed. Your data stays on your device.”

## Logo SVG Starter

Use the SVG file included in the starter package as a base concept. It is intentionally simple so it can be refined later in Figma or Illustrator.

## Accessibility Rules

- Maintain strong color contrast.
- Do not use color alone to show meaning.
- Income and expense should have icon/text difference, not only green/red.
- Touch targets should be comfortable on mobile.
- Charts should include labels and summaries.

## Final Brand Recommendation

Use:

- Name: TrackPaisa
- Tagline: Track every rupee, without the clutter.
- Theme: Green + White + Blue
- Logo: Coin + trend line
- Visual tone: Clean, private, practical, modern
